# Decisions

## 2026-06-24 - Preserve Existing Draft Chapters

Decision: The artifact draft chapters 1-5 are treated as canon and were copied into `manuscript/` as prose-only chapter files. Continuity-note appendices remain source material, not chapter prose.

Reason: The draft already establishes voice, logistics, relationships, and phrase seeds. Replacing it would weaken continuity.

## 2026-06-24 - Use Joel-Survives Home Ending

Decision: The rough draft uses the primary ending path: Joel reaches North Road Bridge and later hears an impossible sound at home.

Reason: It completes the survival arc while preserving ambiguity and leaves the horror in ordinary sound rather than cosmic explanation.

## 2026-06-24 - Final Home Sound

Decision: The final sound is Mike clearing his throat and saying, "We're exactly where we ought to be."

Reason: Mike's phrase carries route confidence, authority, denial, and the whole trip's false premise. It is quieter and more thematic than an Alex grief stinger.

## 2026-06-24 - Mike's Death Cause

Decision: Mike dies at the failed cabin after following gear sounds and false Will/Alex cues while trying to recover the Highlander key and prove the old trace still matters.

Reason: This lets his competence and control flaw kill him without making him foolish.

## 2026-06-24 - Will's Death Cause

Decision: Will dies on the final portage because he tries to test and correct the mimicry aloud, convinced he can force a contradiction.

Reason: His insight is real, but his need to verbalize and answer is the danger.
